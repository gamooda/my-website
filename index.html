Mode History
In distant December 2008, G3H (GeXeH) published his work - the SledBuild game mode for Garry's Mod 10 on the platform "garrysmod.org". The development of this mode gave the author his initial understanding of programming basics.
On January 24, 2013, Robinkooli ported the work as part of the Workshop Beta for Garry's Mod, offering an updated and corrected version of the mode for Garry's Mod 13.
